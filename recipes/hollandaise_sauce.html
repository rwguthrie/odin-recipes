Hollandaise Sauce

Description

It's a rich sauce. It's made of butter and eggs. What more do you want?

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Dijon mustard
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack egg yolks into bowl
  2. Melt butter
  3. Combine everything in a bowl
  4. Whip sauce until it emulsifies
